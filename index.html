About college

Informatics College is an autonomous Private Institution dedicated to maintain excellent academic performance. Since its establishment in 2011, Informatics College has been actively working as a regular franchisee of Informatics Education Limited, Singapore. Informatics College has an established track record of Academic excellence, within the Networks of Informatics – Globally. Informatics College is today producing competitive Graduates, creating an enviable position among employers and Universities both Nationally and Internationally. Informatics College Pokhara, today can proudly boast of having produced sustainable and marketable graduates in various IT and engineering programs.

Pathway Programme

We make learning exclusive for you. We know everyone has different learning needs and lifestyles.
